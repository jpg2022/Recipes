BlueBerry Pie

The Delicious BlueBerry Pie!

So delicious you will never want to stop eating it!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 14.1 ounces double crust pie pastry
  • 1 tablespoon butter
